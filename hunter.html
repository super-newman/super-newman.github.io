Hunter

Face Hunter

Strategy

  • Hit the enemy hero directly to kill them as quickly as possible
  • Use weapons to control the board so your minions stay alive to deal damage
  • Slow the enemy down with secrets

Common Counter Strategies

  • Face Hunter is the fastest deck in the meta, so control the board, even if your deck is normally aggressive
  • Plan around traps; sometimes the correct move is not to attack at all
  • Trade in your minions to avoid strengthening their Unleash the Hounds
  • Silence their Mad Scientists
