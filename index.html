Hearthstone Ladder Guide

This is a quick strategy reference for the various decks popular in the Hearthstone ladder metagame. Obviously, any individual deck may be customized. This website covers the most popular variants of each deck. Cards which are less common in a deck, but still show up fairly often (> 20%, anecdotally speaking), are marked with a question mark (?) preceding the card name. The decks are organized by class. You can use the navbar at the top of the page or the selections below to choose a class.
