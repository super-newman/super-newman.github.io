Druid

Aggro Druid

Strategy

  • Develop strong board presence and damage opponent early
  • Finish opponent with early (possibly Innervated) Fel Reaver, or Force of Nature / Savage Roar combo

Common Counter Strategies

  • Maintain board control
  • Be prepared to deal with Fel Reaver
    • Incapacitating Fel Reaver (freeze, taunt, Aldor Peacekeeper, etc.) and forcing crippling card draw can be effective
  • Stay out of combo range

Egg Druid

Strategy

  • Maximize the number of creatures on their board with stat buffs (e.g. Power of the Wild, Defender of Argus) and sticky buffs (Soul of the Forest) to overwhelm the opponent.
  • Empty hand fast and use Cult Master or Jeeves for card draw.
  • Savage Roar used for mid-game board clear and finishing burst.

Common Counter Strategies

  • Minimize the number of minions they have on their board each turn.
  • Be prepared to empty your own hand in case they play Jeeves.

Midrange Druid

Strategy

  • Outvalue your opponent by speeding your mana curve using Wild Growth and Darnassus Aspirant.
  • Use the Force of Nature / Savage Roar combo for a large finishing burst.

Common Counter Strategies

  • Overwhelm them quickly before they build a board presence
  • Stay out of combo range
  • Play around Swipe
