Warrior

Control Warrior

Strategy

  • Slow the opponent’s advance through heavy use of removal and armor.
  • Overwhelm them in the late game with huge minions.
  • Common finisher is a buffed Grommash Hellscream (triggered same turn for 10+ damage charge), sometimes set up with Alexstrasza.

Common Counter Strategies

  • Defeat them before the late game
  • Keep their armor low to limit their Shield Slams
  • Remove Armorsmiths early
  • Play around brawl
