Mage

Freeze Mage

Strategy

  • Focus on neutralizing all board threats with freeze spells and board clears.
  • Extend the game with Ice Barrier and Ice Block.
  • Kill the opponent with massive burst damage, often set up by Archmage Antonidas, Malygos, and/or Alexstrasza.

Common Counter Strategies

  • Threaten them every turn. This may exhaust their defensive plays, or force them to repurpose their late-game burst spells for defensive purposes.
  • Stretch to pop their Ice Blocks with burst, while taking care to leave their health as close to 1 as possible.
  • Loatheb can be a key play; time it carefully. Strong options include before their turn 5 (to prevent Frost Nova + Doomsayer), when popping an Ice Block, or in response to Alexstrasza.

Tempo Mage

Strategy

  • Control the board early with Mana Wyrm, Mirror Entity, Mirror Image, and removal spells.
  • Magnify impact of removal spells using Sorcerer’s Apprentice and Flamewaker
  • Use Archmage Antonidas for late-game reach

Common Counter Strategies

  • Fight for board control early on -- early minions’ abilities can snowball quickly.
  • Silence Mad Scientist and Flamewaker to slow them down.
  • Be careful of lethal bursts and plan around Antonidas in the late game.
